Cooking Basics

"Cooking is the art, science, and craft of preparing food for consumption by applying heat in various methods." It involves selecting, measuring, and combining ingredients in an ordered procedure to achieve desired flavors, textures, and nutritional value.

How to prepare Tea?

  1. Boil fresh water in a kettle or saucepan.
  2. Warm the teapot or cup by rinsing with hot water (optional).
  3. Add 1 tea bag or 1 tsp loose tea per cup into the teapot or an infuser.
  4. Pour the boiling water over the tea and start timing.
  5. Steep for 2–5 minutes depending on tea type (green ~2–3 min, black ~3–5 min).
  6. Remove the tea bag or strain the leaves to stop brewing.
  7. Add milk, sugar, honey, or lemon to taste (optional).
  8. Stir, pour into cups, and serve hot.
